Kerry James Marshall

06/16

Kerry James Marshall, born in 1955 Birmingham Alabama, received a BFA and an honorary doctorate from the Otis art institute in Los Angeles. The Subject Matter of Marshalls work takes inspiration from black popular culture. References ranging from renaissance paintings to folk art. Marshall is heavily influenced by the geography of his upbringing. “You can’t be born in Birmingham, Alabama, in 1955 and grow up in South Central near the Black Panthers headquarters, and not feel like you’ve got some kind of social responsibility. You can’t move to Watts in 1963 and not speak about it. That determined a lot of where my work was going to go.”
